Welcome

Welcome to
Psalms & Hymns Parish

Psalms & Hymns Parish started with a small gathering of believers and has grown within a short time under the direction of the Holy Spirit. Since inception we have continually benefitted from God's unending favour and grace.

Our Parish is a church for everyone — for ALL irrespective of Race, Class, Age, Gender and Background. We are committed to authentic worship, sound teaching, and serving our community with the love of Christ.

We are connected to a wider fellowship of churches within the United Kingdom, one of the fastest growing Pentecostal networks in the world with parishes across the UK, Europe, USA, Africa, and beyond.
